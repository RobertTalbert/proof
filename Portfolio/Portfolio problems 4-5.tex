\noindent
\textbf{Problem 5A:} Suppose that $a$ and $b$ are natural numbers such that $a^2 = b^3$. Prove that if $4 | b$, then $8 | a$. 

\medskip

\textbf{Problem 5B:} For every integer $a$, prove that if $a^2 - 1$ is even, then $4 | (a^2 - 1)$.
